Currency Configuration

Configure the application's currencies and rendering from this module.

Definitions

  • Symbol: the symbol with which the currency is identified and rendered. Appears after the amount.
  • Separator: the delimiter used to denote the thousands places in an amount.
  • Decimal: the delimiter used between the integer parts of an amount and the decimal parts.

Actions

  • Edit the currency item.
  • Delete the currency item.
