| ERR_TXN_UNRECOGNIZED_DATE    | Unrecognized dates in transaction(s).  Dates do not fall within a valid fiscal year     |
| ERR_TXN_UNRECOGNIZED_DC_UUID | Unrecognized debitor/creditor ID in transaction(s)                                      |
